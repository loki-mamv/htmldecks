Presentation Templates for Pharma & Biotech

Pharma and biotech presentations carry some of the highest stakes in business. Whether you're presenting Phase 2 data to investors, defending your pipeline at a medical conference, or briefing the FDA, your slides need to convey scientific rigor and business competence simultaneously.

Science Meets Storytelling

The best biotech presentations don't just report data — they tell the story of a molecule's journey from lab to patient. Investors need to understand both the science and the commercial opportunity. Regulators need clarity and precision. Conference audiences need to grasp your findings quickly.

HTML Decks gives pharma and biotech teams templates designed for scientific presentations. Clean data layouts, research-appropriate typography, and structured formats that convey the credibility your audience expects.

Pharma & Biotech Presentation Use Cases

  • Investor pitches — Pipeline overview, clinical data, market opportunity, and team
  • Medical conferences — Poster presentations and oral presentation slides
  • Regulatory briefings — IND submissions, advisory committee presentations
  • Board meetings — Program updates, clinical timelines, and financial position
  • Partnership discussions — Out-licensing pitches and co-development proposals
  • Internal R&D reviews — Program updates, data reviews, and decision gates

Data Presentation That Earns Trust

In pharma, data quality determines everything. Sloppy chart formatting, unclear axis labels, or messy tables undermine your credibility — even when the underlying data is strong. Our templates ensure your data is presented with the precision your audience expects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HTML Decks for medical conference presentations?

Absolutely. Our templates include clean layouts for data tables, Kaplan-Meier curves (as images), patient flow diagrams, and study schematics. The format supports the visual rigor that scientific presentations require.

How do biotech startups use HTML Decks for investor pitches?

Biotech founders use our fundraising template with pipeline visualization, clinical timeline, mechanism of action graphics, and market opportunity analysis. The professional format builds investor confidence.

Can I keep confidential trial data secure?

HTML files are local files you control completely. No cloud storage, no third-party servers. Your confidential clinical data stays on your infrastructure.

Do HTML Decks work for poster presentations?

For digital poster sessions, yes. For printed posters, you'd typically use a design tool, but HTML Decks works great for the electronic poster displays that are increasingly common at conferences.
