Presentation Templates for SaaS Companies

SaaS companies live in the browser. Your presentations should too. HTML Decks gives you pitch decks, product demos, and investor updates that share as a link, look sharp on any screen, and don't require your prospect to download anything.

Built for How SaaS Teams Actually Work

Your sales team needs decks they can customize fast. Your CEO needs investor updates that look professional without a designer. Your product team needs launch decks that work in Zoom screen shares. Different needs, same requirement: presentations that look great with minimal effort.

Most SaaS companies bounce between Google Slides (collaborative but generic), Keynote (beautiful but Mac-only), and PowerPoint (universal but ugly). HTML Decks gives you the best of all three: professional design, universal compatibility, and one-link sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I embed product screenshots and GIFs in HTML Decks?

Yes. HTML supports images, GIFs, and even embedded videos. Show your product in action directly in the presentation without external links or attachments.

How do SaaS sales teams use HTML Decks?

Most sales teams use a master deck that reps customize for each prospect. Change the company name, swap relevant case studies, adjust the pricing slide. HTML files are easy to duplicate and modify.

Can I use HTML Decks for product launch announcements?

Yes. Product launch decks are one of our most popular templates. Share internally for alignment, then externally to customers and press — same file works for both.

Do HTML presentations work in Zoom screen shares?

Perfectly. HTML presentations look crisp at any resolution and work in any screen sharing tool — Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, whatever your team uses.
