Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HTML Decks presentations in court?

Yes. HTML files can be displayed on any screen or projector. For court presentations, save the file locally and present from your laptop — no internet needed, no cloud dependency, full control over the display.

Are HTML Decks templates secure enough for confidential client matters?

HTML files are local files you control completely. They don't require internet access or cloud storage. Your confidential information stays on your hardware, not on someone else's server.

Can multiple attorneys at my firm use the same templates?

Yes. Create a branded template with your firm's colors and logo, then share it across the firm. Every attorney produces consistent, professional presentations without starting from scratch.

Do HTML Decks work for CLE presentations?

Very well. CLE presentations need to be clear, structured, and accessible to attorneys who may be reviewing them later. HTML format works on any device and looks professional on conference room projectors.
