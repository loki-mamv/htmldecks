Presentation Templates for Retail

Retail presentations need to sell — to buyers, to investors, to landlords, to your own team. Whether you're pitching a new product line to Nordstrom or rallying staff for holiday season, you need slides that look as good as your merchandise.

Every Meeting Is a Sell

In retail, you're always presenting. Buyer meetings at trade shows. Investor updates with quarterly sales data. Store opening proposals to landlords. Seasonal planning with your merchandising team. Every one of these needs a different deck, and they all need to look polished.

HTML Decks gives retail teams a consistent, professional look across every presentation. Customize with your brand, add product photography, and share via link to anyone on any device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HTML Decks for buyer presentations at trade shows?

Yes — it's one of our most popular retail use cases. Present from a tablet for an interactive experience, or use a laptop for a traditional setup. Works without WiFi once loaded.

How do retail brands present to investors?

Our investor update template includes layouts for same-store sales, unit economics, expansion pipeline, and customer acquisition metrics — the specific KPIs retail investors care about.

Can I include my product catalog in a presentation?

Yes. Use product photography, pricing tables, and collection overviews. HTML supports high-resolution images and responsive layouts that make products look great.

Do HTML Decks work for visual merchandising guides?

Very well. Create standardized display guides with photos, layouts, and brand standards. Share via link so every store location has access to the same visual standards.
