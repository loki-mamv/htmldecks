Frequently Asked Questions

Can I display HTML Decks presentations on shop floor monitors?

Yes. Any display with a browser can show HTML presentations. Full-screen the browser and you have a clean, professional display. Works on smart TVs, industrial monitors, and standard screens.

Do HTML Decks work for safety training presentations?

Very well. Safety training needs to be clear, visual, and accessible to everyone. HTML presentations are easy to read on any screen size and don't require any software — important when training happens in varied locations.

Can I include production charts and metrics?

Yes. Our templates include layouts designed for metrics dashboards and trend charts. Present OEE, quality rates, and production data in clean, visual formats.

How do manufacturing companies share presentations across multiple plants?

HTML files can be hosted on your company intranet, shared via email, or distributed through any file-sharing system. Every plant accesses the same presentation on any available device.
