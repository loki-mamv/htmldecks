Presentation Templates for Food & Beverage

Food and beverage brands sell with their eyes first. Your packaging is beautiful, your photography is professional, but your buyer pitch looks like a generic spreadsheet with a logo. HTML Decks gives F&B brands presentations that match the quality of their products.

Sell the Brand, Not Just the Product

When you're sitting in front of a Whole Foods buyer, you're not just selling a product — you're selling a brand story, a market opportunity, and a margin story. Your deck needs to do all three, and it needs to look as appetizing as the product itself.

HTML Decks templates let food and beverage brands present with the same visual polish they put into their packaging. Full-color product photography, lifestyle imagery, and clean data layouts for the numbers that buyers actually care about.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HTML Decks for retail buyer presentations?

Yes — it's one of the most popular F&B use cases. Include product photography, pricing, margin data, velocity numbers, and your brand story in a polished format that stands out.

Do the templates support food photography?

Absolutely. HTML supports full-resolution images. Our templates include layouts designed for product photography, lifestyle imagery, and packaging shots.

Can I present from a tablet at trade shows?

Yes. HTML presentations work perfectly on iPads and tablets. They work offline once loaded — important at crowded trade shows with unreliable WiFi.

How do CPG brands use HTML Decks for investor pitches?

CPG founders use our fundraising template customized with brand visuals. Key sections: brand story, traction (retail doors, velocity, repeat rate), unit economics, and distribution strategy.
