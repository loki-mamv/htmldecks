Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use HTML Decks for listing presentations?

Yes — listing presentations are one of the most popular real estate use cases. Include your CMA, marketing plan, comparable sales, and personal track record in a polished deck that clients can review on any device.

Do the templates support high-resolution property photos?

Absolutely. HTML supports full-resolution images. Your photos will look as good in the presentation as they do in the original files. Use full-bleed layouts to really show off the property.

Can I send these to clients via text message?

Yes. Share a link that opens directly in their phone's browser. No app to install, no file to download. They tap the link, see your presentation. It's that simple.

How do I customize templates with my brokerage branding?

HTML files are fully editable. Change colors, fonts, and logos to match your brokerage. Once you have your branded version, use it as a starting template for every presentation.
