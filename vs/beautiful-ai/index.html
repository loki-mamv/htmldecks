HTML Decks vs Beautiful.ai

Beautiful.ai promises smart slides that design themselves. And honestly, their AI does a decent job of making things look good. But there's a catch: $12/month minimum, cloud-only, and your presentations exist only as long as you keep paying. HTML Decks is the opposite — one purchase, one file, yours forever.

AI Design vs. Thoughtful Templates

Beautiful.ai's pitch is compelling: let AI handle the design so you can focus on content. And for people who would otherwise create ugly slides, it works. The AI maintains consistent styling and reasonable layouts automatically.

But AI design has limits. It makes safe, generic choices. It can't understand your brand, your audience, or your message. HTML Decks gives you thoughtfully designed templates that you control completely. Less magic, more intentionality.

The Subscription Trap

Beautiful.ai's Pro plan is $12/month. That's $144/year, $720 over 5 years. And if you stop paying, you lose access to your presentations. They become hostage to your ongoing subscription.

HTML Decks is $29 once. The files you create are yours forever. Open them in 10 years, 20 years — HTML isn't going anywhere. That's not just cheaper; it's freedom.

When to Choose HTML Decks

  • You don't want another monthly subscription
  • You want to own your presentation files outright
  • You need offline capability
  • You prefer control over automated design decisions
  • You create presentations you'll need to access years later

When to Choose Beautiful.ai

  • You genuinely struggle with design and want AI help
  • You create many presentations quickly and trust auto-formatting
  • You're okay with ongoing subscription costs
  • You don't mind cloud-only access

The Bottom Line

Beautiful.ai is a clever tool with a clever business model — one that keeps you paying forever. HTML Decks is simpler: buy once, own your files, no strings attached. For most professionals, that's the better deal.
