Do You Need Slidebean's Services?

Slidebean's real business is consulting and design services. The software is partly a lead generator for higher-margin offerings. If you genuinely need someone to design your pitch deck or advise on your fundraise, that's valuable. But if you just need good templates? That's what HTML Decks provides.

When to Choose HTML Decks

  • You want a one-time purchase, not a subscription
  • You can create your own pitch deck without consulting

The Bottom Line

Slidebean has built a smart business around startup fundraising. But their core product — the presentation platform — costs more than HTML Decks and doesn't give you file ownership. For founders who can write their own pitch, HTML Decks is the better value.
